Week 1: Laying the Foundation

Building in public

This week, I started laying the groundwork for Pulse, the new uptime monitoring SaaS.

Architecture Decisions

I decided to go with React and Vite for the frontend to keep things fast, and Express for the backend. Tailwind is being used for styling.

The goal is to build a reliable system that can check endpoints every minute without bogging down the database. I'm exploring Redis for a queueing mechanism to handle the high throughput of uptime checks.

Next Steps

Next week, I plan to finalize the authentication flow and get the first prototype of the dashboard working.
