Jack of All Trades

Master of None

For years, I let the first half of this quote stop me. I thought having interests that spanned from Web Development to Immunology meant I was unfocused. I worried that writing about Financial Independence one day and Sci-Fi Worldbuilding the next would confuse people.

But then I realized that the most interesting innovations happen at the intersections of these fields. I learned the full quote:

"A jack of all trades is a master of none, but oftentimes better than a master of one."

This blog is my documentation of that journey. It is a chronicle of my attempt to become a Polymath.

I am not here to be a guru. I am here to explore the systems that run our world. My current investigation weaves together the Digital world of code, the Biological logic of evolution, the Narrative structures of storytelling, and the Financial mechanisms of independence.

But this list is not a boundary. It is just a starting line. As I learn new things, this blog will evolve with me. The only constant is the curiosity.

What are you a "master of none" in? I'd love to hear about your own intersections.
